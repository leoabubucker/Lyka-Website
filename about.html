About Us

Lyka Web Development (Lyka) was founded in 2022 by Leo, an independent programmer, game and web developer. A current high school student, Leo has a love for computers and computer science.

Lyka is a freelance business committed to providing website development, administration, and maintenance to a personal or business clientele. Lyka believes in the importance of innovation and entrepreneurship, and thus offers personalized, professional, and practical web services all at a reasonable and negotiable price.

Why Choose Us

Full Personalization

Anyone can make a website on Weebly, Squarespace, or any other generic templated website generator. But what makes us different, is our commitment to originality.

Lyka uses only HTML, CSS, and JS (no templates) to allow all our clients to fully personalize their sites. We will be in close contact with our clients to personalize their websites to their liking.

Responsive Design

In 2022, websites are viewed on countless devices with varying sizes, from the tiniest phone to the biggest monitor.

To ensure that your site looks professional on all displays, Lyka uses various implementations of responsive design techniques to dynamically change your website to match the user's display screen.

Search Engine Optimization

Lyka ensures that your site will be easily found through search engines, because after all, if no one sees the website, what's the point of one in the first place.

Thus, Lyka uses sitemaps, meta tags, and Google/Bing search console to optimize your website and its appearance on search engines.

Smooth Animation

While all websites are different, most modern websites feature clean animations to increase user awareness and retention.

That's why Lyka will incorporate subtle but crucial JS and CSS based animations to make your website just that much better.

High Quality Visuals

Lyka procures high quality and relevant visuals to supplement the text information on your website.

Additionally, Lyka allows our clients to send us visuals and we will expertly fit them in to the website.

Free Hosting

At Lyka, we understand that you most likely will not have a domain, but you want to quickly make your website public.

While we highly recommend you purchase a domain to make your website more professional, we will host your website for free on the Netlify domain (.netlify.app) to host your website until a domain is purchased.
